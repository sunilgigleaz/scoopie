About us

Ice cream is a frozen dessert made from cream and ice, with added flavors and sweeteners.
This mixture is quickly frozen while it is stirred continuously in a mixer, so that large ice crystals do not form.
Some ice cream is made with carrageenan, a gum found in seaweed so that it is not sticky and easily eatable.
Nowadays, ice cream comes with different flavors, shapes, colours and textures.
It has a melting point of 0 °C (32 °F).
